Sundar Pichai

CEO of Alphabet Inc. & Google

Sundar Pichai is one of the most inspiring leaders in the world of technology. Born in Chennai, India, he rose from humble beginnings to become the CEO of one of the most influential companies in history — Google, and later its parent company Alphabet Inc.

What I Admire About Him

Sundar Pichai’s journey is a testament to hard work, humility, and vision. From studying metallurgical engineering at IIT Kharagpur to earning an MS from Stanford and an MBA from Wharton, he never stopped learning and evolving.

His leadership style is calm, inclusive, and thoughtful. He believes in empowering his teams and fostering innovation without fear of failure. Despite leading a tech giant, he remains humble and grounded.

Inspirational Quote

"Wear your failure as a badge of honor."
